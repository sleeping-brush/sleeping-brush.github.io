who I am

Hello, I’m Louise — a 21-year-old artist and engineering student living in Zurich, originally from Luxembourg with French roots.

I study mechanical engineering, but I’ve always loved art, languages, and creative expression. I began painting in 2020 and recently returned to it with a new focus during my studies.

I write poetry to understand my emotions, and I hope my paintings and verses help others feel something too.

I also enjoy coding — especially when I can blend creativity and technology to build things that support or inspire people.
